Lasagna

Description

This is a standard lasagna recipe, using minced meat and pasta

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • tomato sauce
  • basil
  • lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. Brown the meat in a frying pan
  2. Mix the meat with the tomato sauce
  3. Layer the pasta sheets with the meat mix
  4. Top with grated cheese
    5. Oven cook at 200C Fan for 35 mins

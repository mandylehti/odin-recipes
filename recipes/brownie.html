Brownie

Description

This is a brownie recipe without nuts

Ingredients

  • Ghirardelli Brownie Mix
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Water
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Set your oven to preheat at 180C Fan
  2. Grease baking tray
  3. Empty the contents of the baking mix into a large mixing bowl
  4. Add the wet ingredients
  5. Using a whisk or hand mixer, stir until well blended
  6. Pour the batter into the baking tray
  7. Bake at 180C for 35-40 mins
